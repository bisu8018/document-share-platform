About Polaris Share

A knowledge sharing platform that allows creators and
curators to interact and share documents

  • Knowledge Sharing Share your knowledge of a wide range of informative materials
  • Rewards Rewards for both content creators and curators
    Creator: Produce knowledge Curator: Express opinions through voting
  • Token Economy Powered by a token economy system based on block-chain
  • Audience Tracking B2B marketers can improve their marketing activities by securing an audience that shows interest in the documents.

4 Reasons to be a Creator

  • Accumulate votes from curators on your documents,
    which you can convert into redeemable rewards.
  • Track lead activity and collect contacts.
    You can check those who are interested in your documents and use them as a marketing guidance.
  • Let people know about you through the documents, and expand your reputation.
    You can be the expert in applicable knowledge.
  • Easy sharing process allows you to share documents of any file format/topics quickly and easily.

Polaris Share Process

  • Creator Produce a knowledge and disclose the documented knowledge.Get rewards from sharing documents
  • Curator Express an opinion by voting documents using tokens they own. Get the compensation for the activity they perform
  • Deck The units used to reward users for sharing and curating. Represent the amount of Deck equivalent to $0.05 (Subject to change)
